\chapter*{前言}

This book grew out of lecture notes developed over several years of linear algebra. The goal is to present the subject in a way that emphasizes intuition and geometric understanding while retaining mathematical rigor.  

The material is intended primarily for undergraduate students, although other readers may find it accessible.
